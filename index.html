What We Offer

Our Core Services

Discuss Your Needs
📋
Cost Audit
Statutory cost audit under the Companies Act & Cost Records Rules. We ensure accurate cost records, compliance filings (CRA-1 to CRA-4), and detailed audit reports.
📊
Management Accounting
Comprehensive MIS reports, product-wise profitability analysis, variance analysis, and activity-based costing to empower data-driven decisions.
⚖️
GST & Indirect Tax
GST registration, return filing, reconciliation, audit support, and advisory for ITC optimisation and anti-profiteering compliance.
🔄
Transfer Pricing
Benchmarking studies, TP documentation, advance pricing agreements, and representation before tax authorities for related-party transactions.
💹
Budgeting & Forecasting
Annual budget preparation, rolling forecasts, cash flow projections, and scenario planning to align financial resources with strategic goals.
🏭
Cost Reduction Studies
Value engineering, process benchmarking, overhead analysis, and operational efficiency projects to drive sustainable cost savings.
🔍
Due Diligence
Financial and operational due diligence for M&A, private equity, and joint ventures — covering cost structures, margins, and hidden liabilities.
📁
Income Tax Compliance
Return filing, tax planning, TDS compliance, advance tax computation, and representation before the Income Tax Department.
🏛️
Project Finance & Appraisal
Detailed project reports (DPRs), techno-economic feasibility studies, and project cost monitoring for banks and financial institutions.
